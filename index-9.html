Reading the market honestly

There is no single published national figure for how many property sales involve a compliance issue. What we do know: municipalities are auditing more aggressively than ever, enforcement cycles are penalising hundreds of properties at a time, and localised surveys have found non-compliance running well above one in three properties.

That points to a substantial, currently unquantified market — and one moving in REBES's favour every year as enforcement intensifies.
